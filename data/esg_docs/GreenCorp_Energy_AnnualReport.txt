GreenCorp Sustainability Report 2025

GreenCorp is committed to achieving carbon neutrality by 2030. Our operations are powered by 100% renewable energy sources, and we have reduced our Scope 1 and Scope 2 emissions by 45% since 2019. We have invested $500 million in wind and solar projects across three continents.

Our supply chain sustainability program ensures that 80% of our Tier 1 suppliers meet our strict environmental standards. We have eliminated single-use plastics from all our corporate facilities and are working toward zero waste to landfill by 2027.

GreenCorp's water stewardship initiative has reduced freshwater consumption by 30% through advanced recycling systems. We have also restored 5,000 acres of natural habitat adjacent to our operating sites.

We believe in transparent reporting and have aligned our disclosures with TCFD, GRI, and SASB frameworks. Our carbon offset portfolio includes verified reforestation and direct air capture projects totaling 2 million tonnes of CO2 equivalent annually.
