EcoEnergy ESG Disclosure 2025

EcoEnergy is a leading provider of sustainable energy solutions. We generated 12 TWh of clean electricity in 2024, enough to power 3 million homes. Our renewable portfolio includes onshore wind, offshore wind, and utility-scale solar across 15 countries.

We have set science-based targets validated by the SBTi, committing to a 50% reduction in absolute Scope 1 and Scope 2 emissions by 2030 from a 2020 baseline. Our Scope 3 emissions inventory covers 95% of our value chain, and we are actively engaging suppliers to set their own reduction targets.

EcoEnergy's biodiversity action plan has resulted in a net positive impact on local ecosystems at 70% of our operational sites. We conduct annual environmental impact assessments and publish the results in full.

Our governance structure includes a dedicated ESG committee at the board level, with executive compensation linked to climate performance metrics. We disclose our lobbying activities and political contributions in accordance with best practice guidelines.
