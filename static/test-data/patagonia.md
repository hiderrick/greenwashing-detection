## 2. Evidence of Strong Environmental Credibility

### a) Certified B Corp with High Transparency

Patagonia is a **Certified B Corporation**, meaning it meets rigorous standards of social and environmental performance, accountability, and transparency.

* Patagonia discloses detailed impact assessments covering governance, workers, community, environment, and customers.
* Certification requires recertification and continuous improvement.

**Source:**
[https://www.bcorporation.net/en-us/find-a-b-corp/company/patagonia-inc/](https://www.bcorporation.net/en-us/find-a-b-corp/company/patagonia-inc/)

---

### b) Profits Legally Directed Toward Climate Action

In 2022, Patagonia’s founder transferred ownership of the company into structures designed so that **all profits not reinvested in the business are directed toward fighting climate change and protecting nature**.

This legal structure significantly reduces incentives for superficial sustainability claims because environmental impact is now tied directly to corporate purpose.

**Source:**
[https://www.patagonia.com/stories/earth-is-now-our-only-shareholder/story-15585.html](https://www.patagonia.com/stories/earth-is-now-our-only-shareholder/story-15585.html)

---

### c) Long-Term 1% for the Planet Commitment

Patagonia co-founded **1% for the Planet** and commits at least 1% of annual sales to environmental organizations worldwide.

To date, Patagonia and its partners have directed **hundreds of millions of dollars** to grassroots environmental groups.

**Source:**
[https://www.onepercentfortheplanet.org/member/patagonia/](https://www.onepercentfortheplanet.org/member/patagonia/)

---

### d) Measurable Emissions Reductions (Not Just Offsets)

Patagonia publicly reports its Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions and emphasizes **direct emissions reductions**, not reliance on offsets as a primary strategy.

Key actions include:

* Transition to renewable electricity in owned and operated facilities.
* Increasing use of recycled and preferred materials.
* Redesigning products to reduce material intensity and extend product life.

**Source:**
[https://www.patagonia.com/impact/](https://www.patagonia.com/impact/)

---

### e) Repair, Reuse, and Circular Economy Leadership

Patagonia operates one of the largest apparel repair programs in the world and actively discourages unnecessary consumption.

Notable initiatives:

* **Worn Wear** resale and repair program
* Free repair guides and low-cost repairs
* Product design focused on durability and modularity

**Source:**
[https://wornwear.patagonia.com/](https://wornwear.patagonia.com/)
